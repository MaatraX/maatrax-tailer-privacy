Privacy Policy for Maatrax Studio

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Photos: Images captured using your device camera for profile pictures and order documentation
  • Customer Data: Names, contact information, and measurements you manually enter
  • Order Information: Details about tailoring orders, measurements, and delivery dates
  • Payment Information: Transaction records processed through Google Play Billing
  • Account Information: Email address and authentication data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide tailoring business management services
  • Store customer measurements and order details
  • Process premium subscription payments
  • Send order reminders and notifications
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Photos: Stored locally on your device and optionally on secure cloud servers
  • Customer Data: Stored on secure servers with encryption
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through Google Play - we do not store payment card details
  • Local Database: Order and customer data stored in encrypted local database on your device

4. Permissions Used

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): To capture photos for profile pictures and order documentation
  • Storage: To save photos locally on your device
  • Internet: To sync data with cloud servers and access online features
  • Notifications: To send order reminders and delivery alerts

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To process payments through Google Play
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of promotional notifications

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
