How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps inside the app to permanently delete your account. Please read the warnings below before proceeding.

  1. Open the app and sign in to your account if needed.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Enter your account password when prompted to confirm.
  5. Once you confirm deletion, your account cannot be recovered.

Important — Export Your Data First

Before you delete your account, export any data you wish to keep from the app (for example: customer details, purchase receipts, payments). After account deletion, you will not be able to restore this data.

If your app provides a data export tool, go to Settings → Export Data and follow the export steps. Save the exported file to your device or cloud storage before deleting your account.
